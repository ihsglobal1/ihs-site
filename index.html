Our Story

Innovative Health Solutions began with a question that wouldn't let go: why do diseases that quietly threaten thousands of lives remain so poorly understood in the very systems meant to protect them?

That question took root during the origin of neglected tropical disease work that combined biostatistics, epidemiology, and years of experience in administrative health datasets. Overall findings were clear: neglected tropical diseases don't stay neglected by accident.

Founder and Principal Consultant Joshua Trowell, PhD, MPH carried that conclusion out of the dissertation defense and into the field after relocating to Port Vila, Vanuatu, to build a consultancy where the work could happen where it mattered most: in a region where climate change, water systems, and vector-borne disease are not separate problems, but one interconnected reality.

IHS was registered in 2026 as a Vanuatu-based public health consultancy, built to bring rigorous epidemiological methodology to the programs, ministries, and partners working to protect Pacific communities from neglected tropical diseases and the climate forces accelerating them.

Today, IHS operates as a full-time, regionally embedded consultancy.

What We Do

Services built for the field, not the slide deck.

Every engagement is grounded in the same standard: methodology rigorous enough for peer review, delivered at a pace that works for ministries, NGOs, and multilateral partners.

01

NTD Program Design

End-to-end design of neglected tropical disease surveillance and intervention programs, from baseline epidemiological assessment and case definition through implementation frameworks, monitoring plans, and donor-ready reporting structures.

02

Climate-Health Integration Consulting

Technical support for governments and partners building climate-health adaptation plans, connecting rising temperatures, shifting rainfall, and extreme weather events directly to disease vectors, water safety, and health system resilience.

03

Proposal & Grant Writing Support

Full proposal development for WHO, UN, and multilateral RFPs to include technical narratives, methodology sections, budgets, and Gantt charts. Additional compliance documentation is built to abide by procurement standard, from concept note through final submission.

04

Training & Talks

Workshops, technical trainings, and keynote presentations for health ministries, conferences, and academic audiences by translating complex epidemiological findings into guidance teams can act on.
